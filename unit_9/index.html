About Us

Rosie HQ is a network of students here to make studying easier. From grade school to grad school we are here to meet your needs. Our mission is to create a safe space for students to learn and expand their knowledge of various subjects with the convenience of technology. Everything from study guides to study groups, Rosie HQ is here for you!

What We Do

We here at Rosie HQ have a great emphasis on community. The tough questions that may be too difficult to ask in the classroom are welcomed here. We want students to feel supported and have a free space to explore their minds. We offer personalized study guides, 1:1 tutoring, and create study groups so that you can learn with and from you peers
